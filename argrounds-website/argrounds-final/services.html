Common Questions

Everything You Need
to Know.

How much does pressure washing cost?

Every job is different — driveway size, surface condition, and service type all affect pricing. That's why we give free quotes upfront. Most residential driveways fall in an affordable range, and our bundle saves you more. Fill out our quote form or text us a photo for the fastest estimate.

How long does a typical job take?

Most residential driveways take 1–2 hours. Adding walkways might add another 30–60 minutes. We'll give you a realistic time estimate when we quote the job.

Do you use hot or cold water?

We use professional-grade equipment with adjustable pressure and temperature. For most concrete surfaces, cold water at high pressure is highly effective. For oil stains or heavy grease, hot water is the better choice — we'll use what's best for your surface.

Will pressure washing damage my concrete or pavers?

Not when done by professionals. We adjust pressure settings for the surface type. Stamped concrete and pavers get a gentler treatment than plain poured concrete. We've cleaned hundreds of driveways without a single damage claim.

How soon can you come out?

We often have same-week availability. Reach out via the form, call, or text and we'll get you scheduled fast. We don't let leads sit — you'll hear from us the same day.

Do I need to be home during the service?

Not necessarily. As long as we can access the driveway and have a water source (usually your outdoor spigot), we can work while you're away. We'll walk you through logistics when we confirm your appointment.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

We stand behind our work 100%. If you're not satisfied, tell us and we'll come back and make it right — no excuses, no hassle.
